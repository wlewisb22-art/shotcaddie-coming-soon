FAQ

Answers to common questions about Shot Caddie.

What is Shot Caddie?

Shot Caddie is a smart, automated shot and cocktail dispenser built to help you load your favorite spirits and mixers, discover what you can make, and pour consistent drinks quickly.

How many bottles can it hold?

Shot Caddie is designed around a 16-bottle system so you can stock a wide range of spirits, liqueurs, juices, and mixers.

Does it support AI?

Shot Caddie is designed to support an optional AI-enabled mixologist experience that can suggest drinks, recommend swaps, and help you discover combinations based on what’s loaded. (AI features may vary at launch.)

Is it only for shots?

Not at all — it’s designed for shots and cocktails. Your recipe library and what you stock in the 16 slots determines what’s available.

How do consistent pours work?

Shot Caddie is built around calibration so timing can be tuned per ingredient (spirits vs. juices) to improve consistency over time.
