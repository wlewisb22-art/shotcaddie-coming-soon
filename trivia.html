Shot Caddie Trivia

Swivel & rotate for everyone

The ring picks. The screen asks. The table answers.

  • The ring picks the player

    An LED strip circles the base, lights spinning around the Caddie until they land on who's up.

  • 7-inch swivel touchscreen

    Tilts up and down for the perfect viewing angle at any table.

  • Rotates 360°

    Turn the screen to face any player so everyone can see the question.

  • Your house rules

    Choose the question categories, set the difficulty, and decide how often a miss costs a drink.

  • Play from your phones

    Prefer handhelds? Join and answer from your phone instead of the touchscreen.

  • Thousands of questions

    A cloud question bank across categories and levels, refreshed periodically so game night never repeats itself.

  • Pours at the ready

    Stainless shot stations ring the base, so every player's pour is staged before the first question.

How a round works

Spin. Ask. Answer — or drink.

  1. Set the table

    Players around the Caddie, pours staged in the ring of shot stations.

  2. Set the rules

    Pick your categories and difficulty, and set how often a miss means a drink.

  3. Watch the light

    The LED ring circles the base and stops on the player who's up.

  4. Answer — or drink

    Get it right and the ring spins on. Miss, and the house rules collect.
