What Shot Caddie is

Shot Caddie is a countertop drink system that helps you stock up to 16 different spirits and mixers, then automatically measures and pours shots and cocktails with repeatable timing and calibration.

It’s built for people who want a clean setup for entertaining: less mess, fewer measuring cups, and an easy way to see what you can make with what’s loaded.

Key features

Stock what you like

Load your preferred spirits, liqueurs, juices, or mixers and label each position so the system knows what’s available.

Make what you can

Shot Caddie can present recipes that match your loaded bottles — including flexible substitutions when it makes sense.

Consistent pours

Calibration-focused design so pours are repeatable and you can tune for different liquids (spirits vs. juices, etc.).

AI-enabled mixologist

Optional AI assistance to suggest shots/cocktails you can make, recommend swaps, and help you discover new combinations.

Fast entertaining

Queue multiple pours and keep the night moving — great for home bars, game rooms, and hosting.

Built to evolve

Designed as a modern hardware + software platform with a web-based interface for setup, recipes, and calibration.

Why people want this

  • Less mess: no sticky measuring tools, fewer spills.
  • Less thinking: the system shows what you can make based on your loaded bottles.
  • Better consistency: repeatable pours after calibration.
  • More fun: quick shots, creative mixes, and AI-assisted discovery (optional).

Note: Always drink responsibly. Shot Caddie is designed for adult beverage dispensing and is not a medical or safety device.
