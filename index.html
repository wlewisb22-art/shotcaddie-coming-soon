Why “Shot Caddie”?

A name with two meanings — and we mean both.

A caddie is the golfer's constant: reading the green, handing over the right club, staying at the player's side through every shot of the round. Shot Caddie brings that same service to the 19th hole, where the shots are friendlier and the reads are easier. One word, two games — and the round isn't over until both are played.
