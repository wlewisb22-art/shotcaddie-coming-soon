Most people get two. The common setup is one Mixer Unit + one Liquor Unit — so everything is organized and mixed drinks are quick and easy.

Standard menu vs Custom menu (boilerplate)

  • Standard Menu: Choose a standard “mixers + liquors” lineup that matches our included recipe board. Bottles and labels are engraved to match the standard menu selections.
  • Custom Menu: Provide your own bottle names (mixers/liquors) and your recipe list. We engrave a custom wood menu board and matching bottle labels.

We’ll add a simple “menu builder” workflow later (upload a list, approve a proof, then we engrave and ship).

How the menu works

The included wood menu lists each drink as pump counts (example: “2 pumps Mixer A + 1 pump Mixer B + 1 pump Liquor C”). To make multiple shots at once, simply multiply the pump counts by the number of shots you want.

What’s in the box

  • Shot Caddie Original base (manual) on a Lazy Susan for easy access to all bottles
  • Either: (A) 8 or 9 vacuum-insulated 500ml stainless steel bottles, or (B) 8 vacuum-insulated 500ml bottles + a shaker to mix drinks in
  • 8 pumps (approximately 14oz each) + storage caps for all bottles
  • Engraved wood menu with instructions and multiple shot/drink menu items
