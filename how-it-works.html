How it works

Load your bottles. Your caddie handles the pour.

One idea runs through the whole lineup: stock the Caddie with the liquors and mixers you actually have, then pour by hand, by machine, or by game.

Manual

Shot Caddie Original

  1. Stock your stations

    Fill the 500 mL glass bottles — eight liquors around the center shaker, eight mixers in two rows of four. Contents are laser-engraved on each bottle.

  2. Read the board

    The engraved menu writes every recipe as pump counts: "2 presses Mixer A + 1 press Liquor C."

  3. Press and pour

    Each press dispenses ¼ oz. Multiply the counts to make several servings at once.

  4. Shake and serve

    Ice in the center shaker, mix, and pour.

Automated

Shot Caddie 2.0

  1. Load sixteen bottles

    Any combination of liquors and mixers, 8–16 oz each. Tell the system what's in every slot.

  2. Browse what's possible

    The menu lists only drinks you can actually make from what's loaded — thousands in the database, plus your own saved recipes.

  3. Pick — or ask

    Choose on the 7-inch touchscreen or your phone, or let the AI suggest a drink based on your inputs.

  4. Walk up and pour

    The multi-line system measures and mixes as it dispenses — no shaker needed. Queued drinks release with a QR code or, optionally, facial recognition.

Game night

Shot Caddie Trivia

  1. Set the table

    Players around the Caddie, pours staged in the ring of shot stations.

  2. Set the rules

    Pick categories and difficulty, and set how often a miss costs a drink.

  3. Watch the light

    The LED ring circles the base and stops on the player who's up. Swivel or rotate the screen so everyone can see the question.

  4. Answer — or drink

    Get it right and the ring spins on. Miss, and the house rules collect. Play on the touchscreen or from your phones.
