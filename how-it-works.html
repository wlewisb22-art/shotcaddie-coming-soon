How Shot Caddie works

Shot Caddie is designed around one idea: load the bottles you actually have, then let the system show what you can make and pour it consistently.

Up to 16 bottles Recipe matching Calibration-focused Optional AI assistance

1) Load your spirits & mixers

Choose up to 16 spirits, liqueurs, juices, and mixers. Assign each slot a label so Shot Caddie knows what’s available. This is the foundation for recipe matching and smart suggestions.

  • Build a “party” setup (vodka/rum/tequila + juices)
  • Build a “cocktail” setup (liqueurs + citrus + mixers)
  • Change bottles quickly without re-learning the whole system

2) See what you can make (recipe matching)

Once your bottles are loaded, Shot Caddie can surface shots and cocktails that match your current inventory. You can also allow practical substitutions (example: swapping a similar rum or vodka flavor when a recipe calls for something specific).

3) Calibrate for consistent pours

Drinks pour differently depending on viscosity and tubing setup. Shot Caddie is built around calibration so you can dial in reliable timing (spirits vs. juices, etc.) and get repeatable pours.

  • Prime and test pour routines
  • Per-ingredient tuning
  • Consistent pour sizes over time

4) Pour now or queue multiple pours

For entertaining, speed matters. Shot Caddie is designed to support quick pours and multi-shot flows so you can keep things moving.

5) Optional AI-enabled mixologist

Want ideas? The AI-enabled experience can suggest recipes you can make from what’s loaded, recommend swaps, and help you discover combinations — while still keeping you in control.

(AI features are optional and may vary at launch. Join the list on the homepage for updates.)
